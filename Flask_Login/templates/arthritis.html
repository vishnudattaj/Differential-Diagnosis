You have: Arthritis

Description:

Arthritis is a condition that causes inflammation in the joints, leading to pain, stiffness, and swelling. There are several types, with the two most common being osteoarthritis (wear and tear on the joints) and rheumatoid arthritis (an autoimmune disease where the immune system attacks the joints). The causes of arthritis can include age, genetics, injury, infections, or immune system dysfunction, depending on the type. It can affect anyone but is more common in older adults.

Symptoms:

  • Joint pain
  • Swelling
  • Stiffness
  • Redness
  • Decreased range of motion
  • Fatigue

Treatment:

Consult your healthcare provider for appropriate treatment options.
