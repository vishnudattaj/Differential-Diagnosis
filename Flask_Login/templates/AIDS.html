You have: AIDS

Description:

AIDS (Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome) is caused by the HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus) attacking and weakening the immune system, specifically the CD4 cells (T cells). This makes the body vulnerable to infections and certain cancers. HIV is transmitted through blood, semen, vaginal fluids, and breast milk. Without treatment, HIV can progress to AIDS.

Symptoms:

  • Frequent infections
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fever
  • Night sweats
  • Chronic diarrhea
  • Fatigue

Treatment:

Exercise: Regular movement to enhance overall health. Stress Reduction: Meditation, yoga, and deep breathing to lower stress. Adequate Sleep: Essential for immune function
