You have: Bronchitis

Description:

Bronchitis is the inflammation of the bronchial tubes, which carry air to the lungs. It is typically caused by viral infections, but can also be triggered by bacterial infections, smoking, or exposure to irritants like pollution or chemicals. There are two main types: acute bronchitis, which is short-term and often follows a cold or respiratory infection, and chronic bronchitis, a long-term condition often caused by smoking. Symptoms include coughing, mucus production, shortness of breath, fatigue, and wheezing. Treatment focuses on managing symptoms and avoiding irritants.

Symptoms:

  • Persistent cough
  • Mucus production
  • Chest discomfort
  • Fatigue
  • Wheezing
  • Shortness of breath

Treatment:

Rest, fluids, and cough suppressants. Inhalers or bronchodilators may be prescribed if symptoms persist.

Ginger & Honey: Reduces inflammation and soothes the throat. Turmeric Milk: Acts as a natural anti-inflammatory. Garlic: Has antimicrobial properties that may help fight infections. Thyme Tea: Helps loosen mucus and ease coughing.

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
