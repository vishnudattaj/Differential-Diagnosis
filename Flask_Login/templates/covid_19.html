You have: Covid-19

Description:

COVID-19 (Coronavirus Disease 2019) is a respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It was first identified in December 2019 and led to a global pandemic. COVID-19 symptoms range from mild to severe and can affect multiple organs. Some people recover quickly, while others develop serious complications like pneumonia or long-term effects (Long COVID).

Symptoms:

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Fatigue
  • Body aches

Causes:

  • Virus: SARS-CoV-2 (a novel coronavirus)
  • Respiratory droplets (coughing, sneezing, speaking)
  • Aerosols in the air (especially indoors)
  • Contact with contaminated surfaces (less common)

Treatment:

Rest Hydration Fever and pain relievers (acetaminophen preferred) Moderate/Severe Cases (Hospital or special treatment): Antiviral medications: Paxlovid (nirmatrelvir + ritonavir) – oral antiviral for early infection in high-risk patients Remdesivir – IV antiviral used in hospitals Anti-inflammatory drugs: Dexamethasone – corticosteroid to reduce lung inflammation Monoclonal antibodies (depending on variants and availability) – used to prevent severe progression early on Oxygen therapy or mechanical ventilation in severe cases

Hydration – drink plenty of fluids Rest – critical for immune system support Warm teas (e.g., ginger, honey, lemon) – soothe sore throat Steam inhalation – can help with nasal congestion Zinc – may help boost immune response (only under guidance) Vitamin D – low vitamin D levels are linked to worse outcomes; supplementation may help (ask your doctor first) Vitamin C – antioxidant support Elderberry – some immune-supportive properties (use with caution)

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
