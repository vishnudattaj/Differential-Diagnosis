You have: Alcoholic Hepatitis

Description:

Alcoholic hepatitis is a liver condition caused by excessive alcohol consumption over time. It leads to inflammation of the liver, which can damage liver cells and impair liver function. Symptoms include jaundice (yellowing of the skin and eyes), abdominal pain, nausea, and fatigue. The severity of the disease can range from mild to life-threatening. Factors like the amount and duration of alcohol use, genetics, and overall liver health play a role in its development.

Symptoms:

  • Jaundice
  • Abdominal pain
  • Loss of appetite
  • Nausea
  • Swelling in the legs and abdomen
  • Fatigue

Treatment:

Avoid alcohol, proper nutrition, corticosteroids in severe cases, and supportive medical care

Stop Alcohol Consumption: The most crucial step for liver recovery. Healthy Diet: High in fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and whole grains to support liver function. Milk Thistle: May help protect liver cells (consult a doctor). Turmeric: Anti-inflammatory properties may aid liver health. Ginger & Garlic: Support detoxification and reduce inflammation. Hydration: Drink plenty of water to flush toxins. Exercise: Improves overall liver function. Probiotics: Support gut and liver health (yogurt, kefir, fermented foods).

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
