You have: Dengue

Description:

COVID-19 (Coronavirus Disease 2019) is a respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It was first identified in December 2019 and led to a global pandemic. COVID-19 symptoms range from mild to severe and can affect multiple organs. Some people recover quickly, while others develop serious complications like pneumonia or long-term effects (Long COVID).

Symptoms:

  • High fever
  • Severe headache
  • Joint and muscle pain
  • Skin rash
  • Bleeding gums or nosebleeds
  • Fatigue

Causes:

  • Dengue virus (flavivirus family)

Treatment:

There’s no specific antiviral treatment for dengue. Care focuses on supportive measures: Pain relievers and fever reducers: Acetaminophen (Tylenol) – safe choice for fever and pain ⚠️ Avoid: NSAIDs like ibuprofen (Advil) and aspirin — these can worsen bleeding. Fluids: Oral rehydration salts (ORS) IV fluids in severe cases to prevent dehydration and shock Hospitalization: Needed for severe dengue (monitoring blood pressure, platelet counts, fluid levels)

Papaya leaf extract – some evidence it may help raise platelet counts (studies ongoing; needs doctor’s supervision) Giloy (Tinospora cordifolia) – traditional herb thought to boost immunity and help reduce fever Coconut water – helps with hydration and electrolyte balance Pomegranate and beetroot juices – rich in antioxidants and may support blood health Turmeric milk – traditional remedy for boosting immunity and reducing inflammation Rest and nutrition – very important for recovery; eat easily digestible, nutritious foods

For more detailed information, consult your healthcare provider.
